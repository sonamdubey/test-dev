Frequently Asked Questions

Before Booking

What is CarWale Advantage?

CarWale Advantage is an exclusive programme only for CarWale users which gives them special advantages when they book their car through CarWale. Through our relationships with more than 1,000 new car dealer outlets across India, we offer exclusive benefits for our users in the form of special savings, complete transparency on prices, assured availability, and more.

How do I book through CarWale Advantage?

Booking a CarWale Advantage car is very simple.

  • 1. Select the particular variant and colour that you wish to book. There may be limited variants and colours available for some cars.
  • 2. Enter your contact details and pay the online booking amount. This amount is fully refundable if you change your mind.

Within a short time, we will re-confirm the availability of the car with the dealer and email you a confirmation of your booking. The brand authorised dealer will contact you to set up an appointment and complete the rest of the formalities at your convenience.

How can CarWale offer me these advantages / benefits / savings / discounts?

We have also signed up more than 1,000 new car dealer outlets as our partners across India. All the cars displayed under CarWale Advantage are 100% brand new. They have not been driven as test drive cars. When the car is delivered to you, it will be brand new and you will be the first owner of this car.

Booking

How can I book online?

Booking online is easy. Just choose your car, choose your variant and colour, and pay the ₹ 2,999/- booking amount online.

Why should I book online? Why book with CarWale? Can’t I get these advantages directly from the dealer?

The special advantages displayed online, including the special savings, are available only through the CarWale Advantage programme. To avail of these, you need to book the car online.

Can I pay offline?

The initial online booking amount needs to be paid online. Only then is the booking registered with these special benefits. All balance amounts can be paid offline via cheque or demand draft. In case you need a loan to finance your purchase, our partner dealer would be happy to assist you with the entire application process and chose a suitable loan plan. You can call us on our Toll Free number 1800-209-0230 if you need any assistance.

Am I booking with CarWale or with a dealer?

CarWale has set up an online platform to accept bookings on behalf of dealers. Your booking is placed with a brand authorised dealership in your city.

Which dealer am I booking with? Can I choose? Why don’t you tell me the name of the dealer before I book?

Once you complete your online booking, our system automatically sends your booking request to a brand authorised dealer in your city who has signed up with us for the CarWale Advantage programme. In order to maximise benefits to our users, the system assigns your booking to that dealer who has that particular car available and is offering the best advantages at that time. Hence you cannot choose your dealer yourself nor can we tell you which dealer will be chosen in advance.

What happens if the chosen dealer is far away from my location?

Most partner dealers will be happy to meet you at your home or office to complete all the booking formalities, including for the car loan if you need one. And once the car is delivered to you, you can take it to any authorised service centre of your choice for service and warranty. Still if you feel uncomfortable about the assigned dealership, we will refund your entire online booking amount at your request.

What if I don’t wish to buy from the dealer assigned to me?

All our partner dealers are genuine brand authorised dealers, and they conduct their business to the highest levels of customer satisfaction as specified by the car manufacturer. Still, if for any reason you are not satisfied with the dealer assigned, we will refund your entire online booking amount at your request.

Which name and number should I mention while booking? Mine or the booking name and number?

You should put in your own name and number. This number is only used to contact you to complete your booking. The actual booking name and number can be specified to the dealership sales executive when they meet you to complete your booking formalities.

Can I book for a different city?

Cars can only be registered for addresses within the city that you are buying in. You cannot buy a car from a dealer in one city for registration / delivery in another city. Please choose a dealer in the particular city where you wish to buy the car. E.g. If you live in Delhi and wish to buy a car in Mumbai, then you should book online with a Mumbai dealer.
At present, CarWale Advantage is available only in Mumbai (including Navi Mumbai and Thane).

Is my booking complete once I pay the online booking amount?

No, the initial online booking amount is only a part of the total booking amount needed. Most cars require an additional booking amount. The dealer will contact you and inform you of the exact booking amount needed to be paid, and they will also visit you to collect the balance payment and get your signature on the booking form.

What if I need a loan?

If you are looking to buy the car on a loan, just inform the dealership sales executive when they visit you to complete the booking formalities. They will assist you with the entire application process and also help you chose a loan plan to suit your needs.

Changes

Can I change my mind about the model, variant or colour later?

You can change your mind at any time before the car registration has been completed. However please note that the special savings, delivery time and other benefits mentioned are applicable only on the specific combination of model, variant and colour chosen, and only on limited stock. Hence with any change in the model, variant and colour, there may also be a change in the final price, delivery time and other benefits.

If the revised price is not acceptable to you, then we will help you with a complete refund of the online booking amount at your request.

Can I change the dealer assigned to me?

No you cannot change the dealer assigned. The special savings and other benefits on offer are available only for bookings through the assigned dealer. If you are facing any difficulties with the dealer assigned to you then please let us know and we will do our best to have those resolved.

You can also always cancel your booking and book again, but CarWale cannot assure you the same special savings or that the same dealer will not be assigned again.

Cancellation and Refund

How do I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking either by calling our helpline at 1800-209-0230 or by informing the dealer. If you cancel by calling our helpline we will inform the dealership of your cancellation request.

Till when can I cancel my booking?

You can cancel your booking any time before the car registration has been completed. Once the car is registered, you cannot cancel your booking.

Will I get 100% refund of my money if I cancel my booking?

The online booking amount is 100% refundable by CarWale till the time the balance booking amount is paid by the customer to the dealer. Post that, all refunds including the online booking amount will have to be claimed directly at the dealership, and will be governed by the agreed upon terms & conditions between the dealer and you, which would be mentioned on the booking form that you sign.

What if the dealer refuses to refund my booking amount?

The payments that you make to the dealership are governed by the terms and conditions agreed upon between you and the dealer. These are mentioned on the booking form that you have signed. Till the time you have paid the entire booking amount to the dealer, CarWale will immediately refund the entire online booking amount that have paid through our website.

Availability and other Timelines

How long does registration and delivery take for ex-stock / in-stock / immediate delivery cars?

Most dealers will be able to arrange registration and delivery within 5 days from receiving 100% payment for the car. However this may change on a case to case basis.

Can I be assured of immediate availability as mentioned online?

Yes. Once the dealer has confirmed the availability of the car, the car will be available immediately, subject to making 100% payment for the car. If you are applying for a loan, then registration and delivery formalities can commence only after your loan is approved.

How long does the loan approval take?

Most banks usually take 7 days to approve a loan after your completed application is submitted. However this may change on a case to case basis.

Do I need to visit the dealership for delivery? Can I get delivery at home?

Some dealers may be able to arrange delivery of the car at your home. However we suggest you experience the joy of taking delivery of your new car at the dealership and driving it home yourself.

Finance and Insurance

I need a loan. Can I get a loan on this car?

Yes. All CarWale Advantage cars are also available on a loan option. Our dealer partner will assist you with the entire application process and also help you customise the loan plan to suit your needs.

What if I want to take the loan from my own bank?

You can avail of the loan from any bank of your choice. Please note that if you are arranging your own loan then you will need to ensure that your bank releases the entire loan amount to the dealership before they proceed with the registration of the car.

What if the benefits include a low EMI / low interest rate / EMI holiday / other loan scheme? Can I arrange my own loan and avail a further discount on the cost of the car?

No. Special finance schemes cannot be swapped for further savings on the price of the car. If you are opting for a loan, we strongly urge you to take advantage of these finance offers to maximise your benefits.

Will the dealer also help with insurance?

Yes the dealer will arrange the necessary insurance as well. The dealership can also offer you multiple insurance plans to choose from. The price displayed includes the premium to be paid for comprehensive insurance for the first year.

What if I wish to purchase my own insurance?

You are free to purchase the insurance on your own as well. In this case the insurance premium amount that was included in the on-road price of the car will be reduced and the balance is payable to the dealer. Please note that you will need to purchase the insurance and provide a copy of the cover note to the dealership before they can deliver the car to you.

What if the benefits include free insurance? Can I arrange my own insurance and avail a further discount on the cost of the car?

No. Free insurance offers cannot be swapped for further savings on the price of the car. We strongly urge you to take advantage of any free insurance offer to maximise your benefits.

Others

Yes. The dealership will inspect your current car and provide a buy back price for your car. This amount will be reduced from the on-road price displayed and you only need to pay the balance amount to them.

Can I buy accessories for the car? Can these be fitted before delivery?

The dealership can provide you with the complete list of approved accessories for the car you are buying. Once you have selected the accessories, the dealership can also have them fitted before delivery depending on their availability. Please note that the on-road price displayed on CarWale includes only standard fitments and does not include any accessories. The price for these accessories will need to be paid to the dealership separately.

Can I buy extended warranty and service plans?

Yes, you can opt for extended warranty plans and service plans that are available from the manufacturer or dealer. Please note that the on-road price displayed on CarWale includes only standard warranty and does not include any extended warranty or service plans, unless indicated otherwise. The price for these plans will need to be paid to the dealership separately.
